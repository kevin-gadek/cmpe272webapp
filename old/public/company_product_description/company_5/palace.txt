This modern, multilevel, hillside home was built with Prairie School architectural style. Its intricate, structurally detailed design contains primarily concrete and steel, including large overhangs with unsupported spans. Other features include: exposed concrete flooring; two-story-high stonework passing through windows; open floor plan with 18-foot ceilings; two-story window walls; cornerless windows; exposed wood ceiling; metal standing roof at barrel vault; and exposed metal staircases.
